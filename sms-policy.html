SMS Policy

Overview
By contacting DFW Signal via phone, website form, or booking a consultation, you agree to receive transactional SMS messages from DFW Signal related to your inquiry, appointment scheduling, or service updates.
How We Collect Consent
Consent is obtained when a customer calls our business number and verbally agrees to receive SMS updates, or when a customer submits their phone number through our website contact form and agrees to receive SMS communication.
Message Frequency
Message frequency varies depending on customer interaction.
Opt-Out & Help
You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP or contact us at info@dfwsignal.com.
Message Type
Messages include appointment confirmations, scheduling updates, and follow-ups related to active inquiries. We do not send unsolicited marketing messages.
Data Use & Privacy
DFW Signal does not sell, rent, or share customer phone numbers or SMS consent with third parties. Phone numbers are used only to provide customer-requested service updates, confirmations, or communication related to active inquiries.
Standard Disclosure
Message & data rates may apply.
