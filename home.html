05 — FAQ

// Common Questions

Straight Answers.

Do customers know it's AI?+
Not unless you tell them. The system uses natural conversation patterns and speaks using your exact business details. If you want to disclose it's automated, we can include that in the script — that's your call.
Can I still answer my own calls?+
Yes — you stay in full control. We configure it so your phone rings through to you first during business hours, and the AI only takes over when you're unavailable, on a job, or after hours.
What happens with emergency calls?+
You set the rules. Route urgent calls to you, send priority texts, or trigger custom logic based on keywords — "no heat," "burst pipe," "power out." It handles emergencies the way you want.
How long does setup take?+
Most setups are live within 3 to 7 business days. We handle everything — scripting, configuration, testing, and onboarding. You just show up to the kickoff call.
Does it handle different accents and slang?+
Yes. Tuned to your actual customer base — regional slang, informal language, background noise. Bilingual support available for Spanish-speaking customers.
Is there a long-term contract?+
No long-term lock-in. We earn your continued business by delivering results — not by trapping you in a contract.
