FAQ

Straight answers.

What's included in the system?+
A custom website, ongoing online activity to keep you visible, a fully configured AI receptionist answering calls 24/7, and access to the Client Portal — your live dashboard for calls, leads, and bookings. Everything managed for you.
Do customers know they're talking to AI?+
Not unless you want them to. The receptionist uses natural conversation and speaks with your exact business details. If you'd prefer to disclose it's automated, we can include that.
Can I still answer my own calls?+
Yes. Your phone rings through to you first during business hours. The AI only takes over when you're unavailable, on a job, or after hours.
What happens with emergency calls?+
You set the rules. Route urgent calls to you, send a priority text, or trigger custom logic based on keywords — "no heat," "burst pipe," "power out." Handled exactly the way you want.
How long does setup take?+
Most setups are live within 3 to 7 business days. We handle everything — website build, receptionist configuration, testing, onboarding. You just show up to the kickoff call.
Is there a long-term contract?+
No lock-in. We earn your continued business by delivering results — not by trapping you in a contract.
